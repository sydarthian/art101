Everyday task list:

Doing eyeliner (Syd’s method)
  1. Buy an eyeliner pen and scotch tape
  2. Sit in front of a mirror
  3. Put a small strip of tape where you want the bottom line of the eyeliner to go.
  4. Draw a line along the tape, then fill in the rest, following the shape of the eye.
  5. Remote tape and apply it to the other eye. Try to place it in the same position as the first eye.
  6. Try to fill in the eyeliner the same as the first eye so they look symmetrical.
  7. Fail to make it symmetrical and redo multiple times.
  8. Feel frustrated.
  9. After several attempts, get them somewhat even and say “good enough”.

Rock Paper Scissors game list:

  1. Display game instructions
  2. Get player input - Prompt the player to choose rock, paper, or scissors.
  3. Validate player input
  4. Generate computer choice - Randomly select rock, paper, or scissors for the computer.
  5. Determine the winner - Compare the player’s choice and the computer’s choice. Apply the rules (rock beats scissors, etc.).
  6. Display the result - Show what both sides chose and who won.
  7. Ask to play again - Allow the player to decide whether to play another round or exit.
  8. Loop or exit - If the player wants to play again, repeat from step 2. Otherwise, end the game with a closing message.
